FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What counts as an interview?

An interview is a single coding session with one candidate. It can last up to 120 minutes. You can pause and resume sessions within 24 hours.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes! Startups with less than $5M in funding qualify for 50% off the Pro plan for the first year. Contact us to apply.

Which AI assistants are available?

All plans include access to Claude and ChatGPT. Pro and Enterprise also include GitHub Copilot. Enterprise customers can integrate custom LLMs.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, encrypt all data at rest and in transit, and never train AI models on your interview data. Enterprise customers can request data residency in specific regions.
